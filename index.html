From September 2022 I will be moving to Imperial College London to join the Imperial-X intiative. I am looking for new PhD students (to start in September 2022). If you are interested please get in touch!

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Warwick at the Operations Research and Information Systems Management departments. Prior to this I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computing at Imperial College London and the Scientific Computing department at CWI Amsterdam.

I obtained my PhD cum laude from the University of Bologna in Financial Mathematics as part of a Marie-Curie ITN-EID project. I have obtained my Master's degree in Quantitative Finance at the VU Amsterdam and my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from the Delft University of Technology.

My research interests are in optimization algorithms and their applications in privacy-preserving machine learning, explainable machine learning, neuroscience and finance.

For our work on last mile logistics using reinforcement learning and optimization with Skialabs, see the website.
