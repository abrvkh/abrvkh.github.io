Anastasia Borovykh

I am currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Warwick at the Operations Research and Information Systems Management departments. Prior to this I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computing at Imperial College London and the Scientific Computing department at CWI Amsterdam. I obtained my PhD cum laude from the University of Bologna in Financial Mathematics as part of a Marie-Curie ITN-EID project. I have obtained my Master's degree in Quantitative Finance at the VU Amsterdam and my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from the Delft University of Technology.

My research interests are in optimization and applied probability and the applications of these topics in machine learning, human-centred systems, and finance.
